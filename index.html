Onn Chong Inc. is a Kelowna-based accounting firm founded in 2006 by Onn Chong, C.A.. Specializing in bookkeeping, accounting, and tax services, the firm supports individuals and small businesses with financial decisions, from mortgage planning to business acquisitions.

The amazing people behind our success

Onn Chong
